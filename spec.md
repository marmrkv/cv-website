## 4) Visual & Interaction Spec (Plain Language)

### Layout & Spacing
- Single-column vertical scroll  
- Generous whitespace  
- Clear section separation  

### Typography
- Clean, modern, highly legible  
- Strong hierarchy  
- Restrained emphasis  

### Color Palette
- Dark background (near-black)  
- Light text with high contrast  
- One restrained accent color for links and CTA

- One primary CTA  
- Minimal secondary links  
- No visual noise  

### Motion
- Subtle scroll transitions  
- Light hover feedback

- Fully readable without zoom

- CTA always accessible
